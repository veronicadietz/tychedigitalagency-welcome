How We Work

The principles behind
every engagement.

01

Our Mission

Find what is structurally wrong and fix it.

Most founders don't have a marketing problem. They have a conversion problem. We locate the load-bearing issue generating most of the downstream drag, and we build the system that resolves it.

02

Our Vision

Marketing that stops manufacturing momentum every week.

A business shouldn't require constant reinvention to keep leads coming in. We build systems that work over time, so the founder gets their energy back for the parts only they can do.

03

Our Values

Diagnosis before prescription. Structure over speed.

We spend real time understanding your business before we recommend anything. We build things that hold up over time rather than things that look impressive on day one. We measure success by conversion, not activity.

Service Agreement

The terms that govern our work.

The full Terms and Conditions live inside your signed agreement. Below is a plain-language summary of what applies to every Tyche client relationship.

  • Scope All deliverables, timelines, and services are defined in your approved proposal. Anything outside that scope requires a separate written estimate and approval before work begins. Additional work is billed at $125/hour or quoted separately.
  • Payment Terms Invoices must be current for work to continue. Invoices more than 7 days overdue may result in a pause of active work. Automatic payment setup is available and recommended for monthly retainers.
  • Refund Policy Due to the strategic and time-based nature of our work, all payments are non-refundable. Once work begins, services, strategy, and intellectual property have already been delivered in part or in full.
  • Revisions Deliverables include up to two reasonable revision rounds related to the original scope. Additional revisions or new requests may be billed separately.
  • Cancellation Monthly engagements require a 30-day written notice. The final 30 days serve as a Close-Out and Handoff Period, with completion of in-progress work, a final review, and transition support.
  • Ownership Upon full payment, final deliverables created for you become your property. Tyche retains the right to reference non-confidential aspects of the project for portfolio or case study purposes unless you request otherwise in writing.
  • Confidentiality Both parties maintain confidentiality regarding proprietary or sensitive information. No disclosure to third parties without written consent.
  • Results We provide professional expertise and strategy aligned with best practices. Because results depend on market conditions, client participation, and factors beyond our control, specific financial outcomes cannot be guaranteed.
